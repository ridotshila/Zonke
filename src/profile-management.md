# Managing Your Profile

After successfully logging in, you can view and edit your profile information to keep your account details current.

---

## Accessing Your Profile

1. 🔐 Log in to your Zonke account
2. 📍 Navigate to **Profile** or **Account Settings**
3. ✏️ Click **"Edit Profile"** to make changes

---

## Profile Fields

Your profile contains the following information. You can update any of these fields as needed:

### Personal Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 📧 Email | Your login identifier (cannot be changed without verification) |
| 👤 First Name | Your given name |
| 👥 Last Name | Your family name/surname |
| ⚥ Gender | Select your gender |
| 🌍 Country | Your country of residence |

### Location Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 🏙️ Town/City | Your city of residence |
| 🗺️ Province/State | Your province or state |
| 📮 Zip/Postal Code | Your postal code |
| ⏰ Time Zone | Your local time zone |

### Professional Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 🎓 Qualifications | Your educational credentials |
| 🛠️ Skills | Your professional competencies |
| 📊 Experience | Your work history and expertise |

---

## How to Update Your Profile

### Step 1: Click "Edit Profile"

Locate and click the **"Edit Profile"** button on your account dashboard.

### Step 2: Modify Your Information

Update any fields you wish to change:

**Personal Details:**
- First Name: Enter your first name
- Last Name: Enter your last name
- Gender: Select from dropdown options
- Country: Select your country

**Location:**
- Town: Enter your city/town
- Province: Enter your province or state
- Zip: Enter your postal code
- Time Zone: Select your time zone

**Professional:**
- Qualifications: Add your degrees, certifications, or training
- Skills: List your technical and soft skills
- Experience: Describe your relevant work experience

### Step 3: Save Changes

After making your updates:
1. Review all changes for accuracy
2. Click the **"Save"** button
3. Wait for confirmation that your profile has been updated
