# Logging In to Zonke

Once you've created your wallet, you can log in to access your account and manage your profile.

## Login Process

### Step 1: Click "In"

On the main interface, click the **"In"** button to begin the login process. This will open the authentication form.

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

You'll need to provide your account credentials:

**Email Address**
- Enter the email you used when creating your wallet
- This serves as your unique identifier

**PIN/Password**
- Enter the system-generated PIN you received during wallet creation
- The field is masked for security

### Step 3: Remember Password Option

You'll see a **"Remember Password"** checkbox. When selected:
- Your login session will persist across browser sessions
- You won't need to re-enter your PIN each time
- ⚠️ **Only use this on trusted, private devices**

### Step 4: Click "Login"

After entering your credentials:
1. Verify your email and PIN are correct
2. Click the **"Login"** button
3. Wait for authentication to process

## Successful Login

Once authenticated, you'll be redirected to your account dashboard where you can:
- View your profile information
- Edit and update your details
- Manage your account settings
- Access Zonke services

## Login Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Forgot your PIN | Use the "Forgot PIN" recovery option |
| Email not recognized | Verify you're using the correct email address |
| Account locked | Contact support after multiple failed attempts |

> 💡 **Tip:** If you're having trouble logging in, ensure caps lock is off and your email is typed correctly.
