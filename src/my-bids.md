# My Bids

Track and manage all your project bids in one place. The **My Bids** section shows all proposals you've submitted for projects on the Zonke platform.

---

## Accessing My Bids

1. 🔐 Log in to your Zonke account
2. 📍 Navigate to **My Bids** from the main menu or visit `/bids`
3. 📋 View all your submitted proposals

---

## Bids Overview

### When You Have No Bids

If you haven't submitted any bids yet, you'll see:

> **No bids yet.**

This message appears when:
- You haven't submitted any proposals
- All your previous bids have been completed or expired
- You're a new user on the platform
